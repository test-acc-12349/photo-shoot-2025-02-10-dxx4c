About Photo-Shoot

Founded in 2020 by a team of professional photographers and product designers, Photo-Shoot emerged from a simple observation: small businesses needed an affordable, professional-quality lighting solution for product photography. What started as a prototype in a garage has grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of entrepreneurs and creators worldwide.

Our mission is to democratize professional product photography by providing innovative, user-friendly tools that help businesses of all sizes create stunning product images. We believe in empowering entrepreneurs with affordable, high-quality solutions that drive real business results. Our core values of innovation, quality, and customer success guide everything we do.
